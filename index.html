2nd Law Brewing, Allen, Texas
Brewmaster: Dr. Dave Catlett
ΔS>0 ... deal with it! ΔS>0 is a statement of the 2nd Law of Thermodynamics. "S" is a property called entropy which is a measure of the tendency of the universe to move toward randomness and low energy. It is the fundamental driving force of the universe, and there's nothing we can do about. So we deal with it ... and have a cold beer.
What we're drinking...
Stargazer Ale is an all-grain Trappist-style ale brewed with pilsner malts and caramel malt. ABV 5.0%. Hard Psider (pronounced just like cider) is fermented pure apple cider with a touch of cinnamon. ABV 9.4%. Do Re Mead is fermented honey from Catlett Apiaries in Mildred, Missouri. ABV 12%. Bottled in August. First tasting in December was very positive. Serve cold in an iced mug!


What's in the works...
Limoncello d'Abruzzo is a digestivo or after-dinner liqueor made from grain alcohol, lemon peels, sugar, and H2O. ABV 45%. Ready to drink by New Years Eve 2020. Dark Matter Stout is an oatmeal stout with 4.1% ABV and hints of chocolate and vanilla. Ready for tasting on January 17, 2021.
